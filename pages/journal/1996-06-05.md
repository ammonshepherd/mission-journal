Today was pretty awesome. It went by pretty quickly. I was still a bit sick. I finally bought some cold medicine and it's been helping a little bit. Everything will go smoothly, I think. I just wish I could get over this dang cold. My nose is raw. Oh yeah, Elder G bleached his hair. I tried it also, but it didn't turn out.
