Today was OK. I got my grüne Ungeheuer bound. That cost me $2.50. I also bought a picture of Ammon defending the flocks. It's awesome. I've always wanted one. I can't think of anything else of importance that might have happened.


    The Grüne Ungeheuer (green monster) was the German language learning book. It was a think stack of paper with a green cardstock cover bound with staples. It was the cool thing to do, to get it bound with a plastic coil binding.
