Today was an OK day. I had to give a talk in sacrament. I prepared it all today, before sacrament. It was only supposed to be five minutes so it was really easy. It was about Priesthood. I also played the piano in Priesthood meeting. I did alright on the first song "Count Your Blessings." The second was going well until the second to the last line on "Sweet Hour of Prayer." It really made me realize how much better I could have been if I would have kept up my practicing. I wish I would have kept up my piano. I don't know if I'll tell Mom that yet.

I saw Elder RK, he had the Westwood journals. But he had just given them to Elder JW a couple minutes before. So hopefully I'll get them tomorrow.

We sang "God Be With You" tonight. It kind of made me mad because we sang it in the English Speaking district. It just made me mad, because we've been here longer but we sang in their room.
