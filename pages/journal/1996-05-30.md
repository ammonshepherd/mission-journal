Heute war ein gute Tag. Der Wetter war sehr komisch. Ein Minuten es war Sonne scheinn, und dann, die nexte minute es war Hageling. What I mean to say, is that the weather was weird today. First was a nice sun shinney day. Then all of a sudden clouds poured in, an it started hailing, big ones, the size of marbles. That only lasted for about an five minutes. That was during the mid-day class. During the evening class, the weather got all cloudy again and it down poured for a few minutes, then turned all sunny again. But just for a few minutes, because then it was nighttime, and darkness fell.

The day went fine for me. I didn't speak as much German as I would have like to. I really need to work on it. It's just frustrating, because I don't know how to say everything that I want to, and nobody else really seems to try too hard. Oh well. Bis Später.
