## Thursday

Today went pretty well. We, as a distrikt, had a little bit of excitement. The Sisters planned out a little joke against us with Elder S. While we were showering Elder S and his comp. placed alarm clocks set for 3:00am and 4:00am, where we couldn't see them. It would have been funny if it wasn't done to us. We, of course, didn't know who had done the dirty deed until lunch time. We kind of got back at them by not talking to them, but Brother C told us not to do anything like that anymore because it was distracting our work in the classroom.

Just before 10:30, Elder R and I had a talk with Elder S about our district, and how can we get the guys to do what their supposed to. He gave us good advice that we've pretty much already followed.
