Germany Hamburg, April 10, 1996 - April 4, 1998
Home Journal

I'm transcribing my mission journal on the day it happened 30 years later.

I wrote in my journal every day on my mission. Well, to be more accurate, if I missed a day, I wrote about it the next day. Every day was accounted for.

Why? I wanted to write a very basic Python static site generator for a while, and since I started my mission 30 years ago, I thought this was a perfect excuse to do that. So I made pysite.

I left out full names and sometimes use a different letter to represent someone's real name. Just in case. I also fixed most grammar issues. My spelling was atrocious. I left most of the mistakes in German grammar and spelling in place, because it's cute, like a little kid learning to talk. And I thought it would be cool to see the progression as I got better. Sometimes I'll add some present day commentary to the bottom of the post.

I wish I would have kept up my piano.

June 09, 1996

Today was an OK day. I had to give a talk in sacrament. I prepared it all today, before sacrament. It was only supposed to be five minutes so it was really easy. It was about Priesthood. I also played the piano in Priesthood meeting. I did alright on the first song "Count Your Blessings." The second was going well until the second to the last line on "Sweet Hour of Prayer." It really made me realize how much better I could have been if I would have kept up my practicing. I wish I would have kept up my piano. I don't know if I'll tell Mom that yet.

I saw Elder RK, he had the Westwood journals. But he had just given them to Elder JW a couple minutes before. So hopefully I'll get them tomorrow.

We sang "God Be With You" tonight. It kind of made me mad because we sang it in the English Speaking district. It just made me mad, because we've been here longer but we sang in their room.
