Mission Journal

Germany Hamburg, April 10, 1996 - April 4, 1998
Home Journal

I'm transcribing my mission journal on the day it happened 30 years later.

I wrote in my journal every day on my mission. Well, to be more accurate, if I missed a day, I wrote about it the next day. Every day was accounted for.

Why? I wanted to write a very basic Python static site generator for a while, and since I started my mission 30 years ago, I thought this was a perfect excuse to do that. So I made pysite.

I left out full names and sometimes use a different letter to represent someone's real name. Just in case. I also fixed most grammar issues. My spelling was atrocious. I left most of the mistakes in German grammar and spelling in place, because it's cute, like a little kid learning to talk. And I thought it would be cool to see the progression as I got better. Sometimes I'll add some present day commentary to the bottom of the post.

My first trip to Aldi!

June 26, 1996

P-Day again. I surprised myself and was able to get up at 6am. I decided to make hash browns this morning, so I did. I started on them about viertel vor 7 (6:45am) and finished viertel nach 8 (8:15am). I don't think they turned out too well. They were all gooey and stuck together. They tasted alright, edible. By the time I was done eating, it was time to get ready to go shopping.

Today we went with Bruder P. He took us to Aldi. He was a little made because we, actually the other Elders, were 15 minutes late getting out to the car. Anyhow, that and buying stamps and mailing packages, took all my money for the rest of the month.

After shopping, I took a two hour nap. It was much needed. After that I wrote a quick letter to Elders F and F, and got their pictures ready to send off. Elder C suggested that we run to the post. I stupidly said OK. So we ran there, I paid 6DM, and then we ran, non-stop, all the way home. that just about killed me. On top of that, though, 20 minutes after we got back, we, all four Elders, went and played b-ball with J at the Roller. That was pretty fun. I still suck. After b-ball, I wrote a letter home and a letter to C. That took me an hour and a half somehow. Either that or I forgot what I did. Then we went and visited, or tried to visit, an old lady that said we could visit her. She wasn't there of course. Then we went on our way to Quedlinburg Str. Not too much luck there. We did get a door open in the place where there never was before. We did quite a few more on Quedlinburg Str. with no luck. Then we went to some other place, I don't know the name of the street. We did a few Tür zu Tür there with a long visit with an old man, and an OK to come back in 3 weeks. So the Tür zu Tür wasn't a total waste. Neither was the whole day. I got a lot of exercise in. I wrote a letter to the family and one to C. And I finally mailed F and F their pictures.

All of my money is gone, and so is my energy. I hope I sleep long and hard tonight. I hope everything is going well at home, with the wedding and all. Only three more days until I'll have a new sister. And she'll be older than me, no diapers. An already grown, older sister. A girl married to my brother. She's cool, though. I wonder if they'll have a kid when I get home. I'm tired, I think I'll go to sleep.
