April 16, 1996. Day 7 - MTC

I still haven't received any Briefe [letters]. I'm not the only one, though. Mein Mitarbeiter auch haben keine Briefe [My companion also has no letters]. Auch [also] Elder N. I learned a lot today in mein Klasse [my class]. I learned how to structure sentences with dative cases. It's neat how quickly I can learn, or comprehend this stuff. It kind of feels like my classes are just for learning German and not any discussions or any other missionary stuff. Today, though, was really neat. We did a lot of neat missionary stuff. It seemed as if we emphasized more on missionary work. We had an exercise in Br. S.'s class where we and another district went outside and pretended we were missionaries and investigators, and worked on our approaches. I sang in the choir in the devotional. It was an awesome experience.
