HOME JOURNAL

1996-04-26

Friday

This morning we went to the Temple at 5:30 in the morning. It was hard to stay awake, bu was a neat experience. Right after that, 8:00, we had a song practice for the conference. We were in there till 12:30. It was so awesome. There were 3 Apostles there with Pres. Hinkley in his office. OUr song, "The Spirit of God", was awesome. I could really feel the power of the Spirit during the song. After that we had lunch and then we did some sleeping and stuff. I also got a letter from A & H. That really made me happy. After Elder R and I got our heads shaved, not really but almost, we ate a quick dinner. Then we were off to class, where we just screwed around, because there was no teacher. But worst of all Elder G left for home.
