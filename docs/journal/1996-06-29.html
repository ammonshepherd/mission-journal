Picking strawberries and listening to the Cure

Saturday, Jun 29, 1996
Location: Halberstadt

Today was quite unusual. One reason was because i was in normal clothes for most of the day. First thing this morning we had to do was visit Bruder J. After that we helped Schwester B's Schwester move. It was a good thing we went to Bruder J's in normal clothes, because we helped him move a big chest thing.

After we helped Schwester B's Schwester, we went to B's to help her and her friend pick Erdbeeren (strawberries). (The reason I didn't say more about "moving" is because I'm going to try and forget all about it.) Well, we ate lunch at B's and listened to the Cure. That was cool. We also played ping-pong. B bought us a kids game of ping-pong for a gift. Then we picked a few Erdbeers. We were cutting time pretty close; we had a Termin with Herr R in Quedlinburg. After picking, we quickly came home and changed, and B drove us to Quedlinburg. We got there just at 18:00. Of course, and I don't know why it's still surprising and disappointing, but Herr R didn't show up at the statue. So we just visited with the Klinks, instead of having a Lektion taught there. I played with Martin for a while, too.

Then we took the Zug to Halberstadt and B picked us up at the Bahnhof. And that was the whole day. It seemed like we didn't do too many things, but I am dead tired.
