Going to Disneyland?

Sunday, May 26, 1996
Location: MTC

The day went pretty well. Elder F left this morning, so I said goodbye to him. It was cool, yesterday, Edlers F and F with their companions came over there to give me some film to develop for them, and to say goodbye. We did a lot of good talking, about how to build a better toilet. We also decided that we're all going to Disneyland when we get back. We'll wear the short Lederhosen and act like German tourists. We'll do it for three days, and each day act like it's our last, and hand around chicks so we can get action or something. I hope we really do that. Not so much hitting on chicks, but hooking up and going to Disneyland. 

    We never went to Disneyland. And I barely hung out with Edler F at all. And I never saw the other Elder F again. And I've actually never been to Disneyland California since I was a child.
