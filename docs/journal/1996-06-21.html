Lots of Tür zu Tür

Friday, Jun 21, 1996
Location: Halberstadt

Today wasn't normal, but it wasn't weird like yesterday. First off, I slept in. Then at 9:30, we had song practice with Brüder J, B, Elder C and I. We were practicing for U's birthday, which we are singing for. Then we, Elder C and I, stayed in the apartment so Elder C could finish his lesson for Distrikts Versammlung. I was able to make the refried beans and read 1 Nephi 13-14. I found out some cool things, not really found out, but just had deeper insight.

Then we did a little Tür zu Tür and almost gave out a Book of Mormon before that. Then we taught the 1st discussion to a lady on Ludsawstraße and her daughter. It went pretty well. They kept the Book of Mormon and the daughter said she would read the things, but the mother wouldn't. They were both really nice. Then we did some more Tür zu Tür. We made three appointments for next week. I sure hope they work out. We did Tür zu Tür until 20:21. My feet and legs are tired, so it was a good day.
