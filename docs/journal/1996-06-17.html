Mission Journal

Germany Hamburg, April 10, 1996 - April 4, 1998
HOME JOURNAL

Monday, Jun 17, 1996
Location: Halberstadt

Today was really good at the beginning. On our way to the Bahnhoff (train station) we got pictures with the LDS license plate, and best of all gave out a Book of Mormon. It was awesome. We just barely made it to the Zug (train) on time. We spent pretty much the whole day in Quedlinburg. we had a Mittagsessentermin (lunch appointment) with a sort of investigator couple. We ended up staying there for two hours, most of it answering the man's questions. It was cool. I also bought some paper, so now I can write home. Then we did some door to door with no luck. On the way to visit the N's we almost gave out two [copies of the] Book of Mormon. for the whole day, we gave out one and almost gave out three or four. We were almost late for the train back to Halberstadt because we talked with the N's too long. We had then an appointment with Herr S. But he wasn't there. That made me so disappointed. On the way home we talked with a few people, and were laughed at and made fun of by kids. That doesn't matter though, this Church is still true. I hope tomorrow will be better. Especially on my feet. They're killing me. Hopefully I'll get used to it.
