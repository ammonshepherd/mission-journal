Mission Journal

Germany Hamburg, April 10, 1996 - April 4, 1998
HOME JOURNAL

Seeing friends from home

Saturday, May 11, 1996
Location: MTC

Today went by pretty normally. I still felt a little weak, but was able to make it to all my classes. Elder J also felt pretty bad and Sister H was getting pretty sick also. Our personal study was real effective for all of us. I was able to get all the verbs we've learned so far that I didn't know all together in a new spiral note pad. Now I just need to get all the nouns and what not into a separate book.

Also something I forgot to mention. On Wednesday B. L. came in. He's the cousin of T. and A. K. who came over in the summer a couple of times. He's going to Hamburg also. It was neat to see him. I had thought he was coming in after I left, so I was surprised to see him.

One cool thing is that D. F., Elder F and his district moved into the room across the hall from mine. They were in that building to begin with but got moved out and now they're back in. Now I see him more than I used to. He's cool still. He and another Elder in his district are always together and talking with me. They're cool.
