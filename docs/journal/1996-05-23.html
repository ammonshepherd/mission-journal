The best day so far!

Thursday, May 23, 1996
Location: MTC

Today was probably one of, if not, the best day I've had so far. What made that so was a letter that I got from A. He was writing in response to the letter I had sent him about how I felt that the Elders were treating me as a tag along and an outcast and what not. It was just an awesome letter. I love him so much. He's my bestest best friend. He said he'd like to come up here to see me off at the airport. I hope he does, that would be awesome.
