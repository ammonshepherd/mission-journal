First full day in Germany!

Friday, Jun 14, 1996
Location: Halberstadt

Heute war mein erster ganzer Tag. Wow! I walked through half the city, it felt like. I did quite a few street contacts. I can't wait until I can understand everything that people say. For street contacts, all I do is introduce me and my companion and say we have a message or something. I can't really understand the reply, so my comp answers. We also did door to door. We must have tried a hundred doors. No joke! We have one Termin for Montag or Dienstag. I hope that goes well. My feet and knees hurt! We did a lot of walking.

I kind of wish R hadn't given me a picture of herself. She's so beautiful, every time I see her, it stabs my heart. Oh well. I won't dwell on that anymore. Tomorrow should be a litte bit easier.
